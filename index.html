Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

Do you travel to all areas of Aruba?
Yes — we serve events across the entire island. See the Service Areas in our Contact section for examples.
What languages do you speak during events?
We speak English, Papiamento, Spanish, and Dutch.
Can you work around our venue's schedule?
Absolutely. We offer flexible hours — early mornings to late nights — and coordinate closely with your venue.
How does the Magic Mirror actually work?
Our interactive mirror greets guests, guides poses and mini‑games, captures photos with professional lighting, and enables instant sharing via QR or email.
What's included in setup and breakdown?
We handle full delivery, setup, on‑site operation, and breakdown. An attendant is present to assist guests and ensure everything runs smoothly.
Do you work with other vendors?
Yes — we love collaborating with planners, venues, DJs, and photographers to fit seamlessly into your event.
What's the difference between packages?
Digital‑Only focuses on the full mirror experience with digital delivery. Standard adds unlimited photos/GIFs, fun props, instant sharing, online access, and custom borders. Premium includes everything in Standard plus professional event photography.
How far in advance should we book?
We recommend booking as early as possible — weekends and peak dates fill quickly. If your event is soon, contact us to check availability.
